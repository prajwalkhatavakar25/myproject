Banking Basics

Understand how banks work, the types of accounts available, and how to use digital banking safely and effectively.

What is a Bank?

  • Accepts deposits: Keeps money safe and pays interest.
  • Provides loans: For homes, cars, education, and businesses.
  • Enables payments: UPI, NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, cheques, cards.
  • Other services: Fixed/Recurring deposits, lockers, forex, investments.

Common Account Types

  • Savings Account: For saving and earning interest.
  • Current Account: For businesses; unlimited transactions.
  • Fixed Deposit (FD): Locked funds for a fixed return.
  • Recurring Deposit (RD): Monthly savings with fixed returns.
  • Special Accounts: Salary, student, or basic accounts.

Choosing a Bank

  • Reputation and stability.
  • Fees and charges.
  • Interest rates.
  • Convenience (ATMs, branches, online banking).
  • Customer service quality.

Loans & Credit

  • Personal Loan: Flexible, higher rates.
  • Home Loan: Property purchase, tax benefits.
  • Auto Loan: Vehicle financing.
  • Education Loan: Study funding, with moratoriums.
  • Credit Score: Affects loan approvals and interest rates.

Safety & Fraud Prevention

  • Use strong passwords and enable 2FA.
  • Monitor accounts regularly.
  • Avoid phishing scams.
  • Report suspicious activity quickly.

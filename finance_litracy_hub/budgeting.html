Budgeting Basics

Learn how to plan, track, and manage your money effectively to achieve financial stability and reach your goals.

Understanding Budgeting

Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to manage your income and expenses. It helps you track where your money goes and ensures that you have enough funds for essential needs and future goals.

Steps to Create a Budget:

  • Calculate your total monthly income.
  • List all your monthly expenses, including fixed and variable costs.
  • Set financial goals, such as saving for emergencies or paying off debt.
  • Spend money based on your priorities.
  • Review and adjust your budget regularly to stay on track.

Loans Basics

Understand loans, their types, terms, benefits, and risks. Learn how to borrow wisely and manage repayments effectively.

What is a Loan?

A loan is money borrowed from a bank, financial institution, or individual that must be paid back with interest over a certain period of time.

Types of Loans

  • Personal Loan: For needs like medical bills, education, or travel.
  • Home Loan: To buy or build a house.
  • Education Loan: To pay for higher education.
  • Car/Vehicle Loan: To buy a car, bike, or other vehicle.
  • Business Loan: To start or expand a business.
  • Gold/Collateral Loan: Money borrowed by pledging gold or assets.

Important Loan Terms

  • Principal: The original amount borrowed.
  • Interest: Extra money you pay to the lender for using their money.
  • Tenure: The time period to repay the loan.
  • EMI (Equated Monthly Installment): Fixed monthly payment of principal + interest.
  • Collateral: Asset pledged for security (not required for all loans).

Pros of Loans

  • Helps achieve big goals (education, home, business).
  • Provides immediate funds when needed.
  • Flexible repayment options.

Cons of Loans

  • High interest rates may increase cost.
  • Late payments can affect your credit score.
  • Risk of losing assets if collateral is used.

Tips Before Taking a Loan

  • Borrow only what you can repay.
  • Compare interest rates from different banks.
  • Read terms and conditions carefully.
  • Always pay EMIs on time to avoid penalties.
